Taiwan's Largest Tea Region

Located in Mingjian Township, Nantou County, Songboling is Taiwan's largest tea-producing region. Blessed with ideal climate conditions, it nurtures the renowned "Songboling Evergreen Tea". Beyond tea culture, this area features the majestic Shotentien Temple, beautiful mountain trails, and rich ecological resources.
